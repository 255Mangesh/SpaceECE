start your journey to a better life with our courses

'MS Talks series on ECE' is a joint effort of two organizations namely, 'MERIT Centre' and 'SPACE for ECE'. Organizers, Dr Balkrishna Bokil, Mr Sachin Mohite and Ms Aparna Chaudhari have vast experience in the Education space and specifically in child development, care, and education. MS TALKS are conducted periodically, which organizes knowledge sharing sessions by experts. It has also managed to develop a virtual community-of-practitioners who gather every week to discuss various child development aspects. It also became a medium of Continuous Professional Development for Early childhood Education educators.
